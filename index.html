Our Story

Hi, I'm Emma Rodriguez, the founder and head baker of Sweet Dreams Bakery. What started as a childhood passion for baking with my grandmother has blossomed into a dream come true.

After years of perfecting family recipes and learning from master bakers around the world, I opened Sweet Dreams in 2019. Our mission is simple: to bring joy to your day through the art of exceptional baking.

Every croissant, every loaf, every pastry is made by hand with the finest ingredients. We believe that great baking takes time, patience, and most importantly, love.
